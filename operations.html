Operations

Start A New Chapter

If you would like to expand and bring COVID Networks to your area, then we encourage you to fill out this form and start a COVID Networks chapter in your region. By forming a new chapter, you will be responsible for the day to day operations of our organization in your area which includes, but is not limited to, communicating with senior centers, finding new volunteers, and branching out. This is also a great way to gain leadership and teamwork skills.

Partnerships Information

To become a partner of our organization, you can fill out the form here on our website. The executive team will then take a look at the form and reach out to you with more information and a meeting will be scheduled with you. Once a partnership is formed, we will provide you with all our resources such as events, volunteers, and contacts to schedule several types of activities.
